Shore Excursion In St. Peterburg

Our basic shore excursion comprises the following services: a licensed guide, driver, car with air-conditioning, a ticket entitling you to visit the city without the need for a visa, and port fees. The software on our website makes it possible for you to create and order an individual tour yourself – based on your own interests. To do so, go to the page for individual tours (up to 6 persons) or group tours (more than 6 persons), fill in point 1; in point 3 indicate, if you wish, additional places that you wish to visit; and then look at the price quoted for your excursion (meals not included). The final step is to fill in the form and send off your request.
